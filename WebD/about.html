About Spotify

Spotify is a music streaming service that was founded in 2006 by Daniel Ek and Martin Lorentzon in Stockholm, Sweden. It has since become one of the most popular and widely used music streaming platforms globally. Here's a brief history of Spotify:

  • Founding (2006): Founded in Sweden by Daniel Ek and Martin Lorentzon.
  • Launch and Expansion (2008-2011): Launched in Europe and expanded to the U.S. in 2011.
  • Mobile Apps and Partnerships (2011-2015): Introduced mobile apps and formed key partnerships.
  • IPO and Podcast Emphasis (2018-2019): Went public and emphasized podcasts.
  • Ongoing Growth and Innovation: Continues to expand globally, introduce new features, and face competition.
